Overview

Stardew Valley is a pretty fun game and I have countless hours on said game. I played the game for two years and those were some fun times, I've played with frinds cause it was mutiplayer of course, but single player was were its at cause I was able to do thing fast and slow but in multiplayer you have to go at the same pace.

Pros

  • Vast amount of things to do, Multi/Single player, fun exploits.

Cons

  • Once you start progressing fast the game feels too short.
